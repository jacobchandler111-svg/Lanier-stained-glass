CUSTOM STAINED GLASS DESIGN
in Buford, GA

A custom panel begins as a conversation. What's the room, what's the light, what's the story you want the glass to tell. From there we sketch, refine, choose glass with you in the studio, fabricate by hand, and install — the same craftsman from first sketch to final piece of came.

WHAT'S INCLUDED

  • · Free in-home or on-site initial consultation
  • · Hand-drawn cartoons and color sketches for your approval
  • · In-studio glass selection — you can see and touch the actual sheets
  • · Hand-cut, leaded, and soldered fabrication
  • · Final installation with weather-tight finishing

OUR PROCESS

  1. Consultation. We meet, you describe what you want, we measure.
  2. Design. We share sketches and glass samples until you're happy.
  3. Fabrication. Cut, leaded, soldered, and finished in our Buford studio.
  4. Installation. We install the panel and walk you through care.

FREQUENTLY ASKED

How long does a custom panel take?
Most residential commissions run 6–12 weeks from approved design to installation. Larger or more intricate panels can take longer. We share a realistic timeline once we've sketched the design with you.
What does a custom panel cost?
Pricing depends on size, glass selection, and design complexity. After a free initial conversation we provide a written estimate before any work begins.
Can you match an existing window or style?
Yes. We routinely design new panels that complement existing leaded windows in a home or chapel — matching glass type, palette, and pattern weight.
Do you handle installation, or just fabrication?
Both. The same hands that design and fabricate the panel install it. There is no hand-off to a third-party installer.
