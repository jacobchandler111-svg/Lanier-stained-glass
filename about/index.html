The Craftsman

A LIFE AT THE BENCH

Ricky Dorsey founded Lanier Stained Glass to serve North Georgia with the kind of slow, careful work the craft demands. After more than twenty years cutting glass, soldering came, and easing century-old panels back into their frames, the work is still personal — every commission is sketched, fabricated, and installed by the same set of hands.

The studio sits in Buford, but the work travels. From sanctuaries in Atlanta and Macon to chapels in small Georgia towns, Ricky has been entrusted with windows that congregations have looked through for generations.

Our Approach

DESIGN TO INSTALLATION

We handle every stage in-house: the first sketch with you over the kitchen table or in the sanctuary, the cartoon laid out on the studio floor, the glass selected pane by pane, the leading and soldering, and the final installation. There's no hand-off, no subcontractor — the person who designs your window is the person who installs it.

For restoration projects, that means careful documentation before anything is touched, period-correct materials, and reversible methods so future caretakers can build on the work rather than undo it.
